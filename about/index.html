About HolyClicker:


HolyClicker is developed by TTheHolyOne. It is a remake of Cookie Clicker!




How to Play HolyClicker:


To earn cookies all you need to do is click the cookie in the middle of the screen!
If you go inside of shop and buy Auto Clicker for 1000 Cookies it will automatically click every 2.5 seconds!
In shop there is a option called Cheats if you click that you will get infinite coins!
It automatically saves but justr to be safe before you log off click Load/Save Data button!
If you ever want to restart for some reason there is a button to reset as well!
On the screen you will see all your stats as well!
